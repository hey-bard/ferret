Ferret

A fun way to find something new!

Install in 5 minutes!

Download and put the ferret folder on your website. Use this HTML:

<script src="ferret/script.js"></script>
<script>new Ferret(SETTINGS);</script>

To customize your Ferret, look at the code below!

<script>
new Ferret({

});
</script>
I contacted every creator for permission to use their imagery in this demo (except where images were already provided for the express purpose), but I didn't hear back from everyone. If you didn't receive/reply to my message and want your button removed, please contact me!
